Basted Eggs

Easy and delicious basted eggs!

Imagine your favourite sunny side up fried egg and poached egg had a baby. It would be named Basted egg, and it would be just as good as either parent!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbspn Salted butter
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1 Tbspn Water
  • 1/8 Tspn Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Pinch sea salt

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a nonstick skillet over medium heat
  2. Crack eggs into the skillet and let cook until whited have almost set, about 1 minute.
  3. Add water, cover skilled with lid and reduce heat to medium-low.
  4. Remove lid and transfer eggs to a serving plate. Garnish with salt.
  5. Serve immediately!
