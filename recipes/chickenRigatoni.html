Garlic Chicken Rigatoni

Delicious and creamy Garlic Chicken Rigatoni!

This delicious Garlic Chicken Rigatoni is super easy to whip up and is sure to have everyone asking for the recipe! It feeds the whole family and is even better the next day for lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of Rigatoni Pasta
  • 500g Skinless, boneless chicken breasts (diced)
  • 1 Tablespoon italian seasoning
  • 3/4 Cup of water
  • 1/3 Cup fresh sliced mushroom
  • 1/4 Cup chopped marinated artichoke hearts
  • 3 Cloves of minced garlic
  • 1/4 Cup heavy whipping cream

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot with water and 1 tablespoon of salt to a boil. Cook Rigatoni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 minutes. Drain and reserve some of the pasta water.
  2. Meanwhile, combine salt, Italian seasoning and black peper in a bowl. Add chicken pieces and toss to coat.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet until it shimmers. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. You might be tempted to add more olive oil or butter, DON'T do this.
  4. Pour in reserved pasta water and add mushrooms, artichoke hearts, sun-dried tomatoes, and garlic. Cook about 5 minutes.
  5. Mix in cream and cheese. Whisk until sauce is slightly thickened. Add rigatoni and toss to combine.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
